ACCOMMODATIONS

Discover comfortable and convenient accommodations in Tarlac, where a variety of hotels that cater to every traveler’s needs. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, Tarlac offers a relaxing stay with easy access to the province's scenic spots, historical landmarks, and culinary delights.

Luisita Central Park Hotel

Luisita Business Park, Hacienda Luisita, San Miguel, Tarlac City

Luisita Central Park Hotel offers modern comfort and convenience in the heart of Tarlac. Located within the Luisita Business Park, the hotel features stylish rooms, a swimming pool, fitness center, and on-site dining. Guests enjoy easy access to the Luisita Golf and Country Club, shopping areas, and local attractions, making it an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers seeking a relaxing and well-connected stay.

L Square Hotel

MacArthur Hwy, Barangay San Rafael, Tarlac City

L Square Hotel is a stylish 3-star boutique hotel in Tarlac City, offering well-appointed rooms, free Wi-Fi, buffet breakfast, and shuttle service. Guests can enjoy Café Teodora, A Lounge Music Bar, and spacious event venues, perfect for gatherings or corporate functions. Pet-friendly and centrally located, it’s a convenient and comfortable choice for both business and leisure travelers.


Contact Details:

L Square Hotel
(045) 491 0714

Asiaten Hotel

18 Don Gregorio Avenue San Sebastian Village, Brgy. San Sebastian, Tarlac City

Asiaten Hotel offers simple, budget-friendly comfort in the heart of Tarlac City. Guests can enjoy clean, air-conditioned rooms, free Wi-Fi, and on-site parking, along with a convenient in-house restaurant. Just minutes from malls and local attractions, it’s a great choice for travelers seeking a relaxed and affordable stay.

Microtel by Wyndham Tarlac

Hacienda Luisita, San Miguel, Tarlac City

This 50-room hotel is perfect for travelers who are looking for a simple and streamlined experience with its comfortable rooms, thoughtful amenities and warm hospitality. Breath the fresh country air as you explore a number of local destinations. For more fun and adventure, practice your swing at nearby Luisita Golf & Country Club, enjoy outdoor activities, or discover the city’s growing culinary scene.

Achitel Tarlac

Lot 10 Gate 3, Block 52 Earth Street, San Sebastian, Tarlac City

Achitel Tarlac is a modern 2-star aparthotel located in San Sebastian, Tarlac City. It offers air-conditioned rooms equipped with private bathrooms, flat-screen TVs, refrigerators, microwaves, electric kettles, and free Wi-Fi. Guests benefit from 24-hour front desk service, room service, and complimentary on-site parking. Situated just a short drive from SM City Tarlac and Isdaan Village, Achitel provides a quiet yet convenient base for travelers seeking comfort and accessibility in the city.

La Maja Rica Hotel

MacArthur Highway Corner Ligtasan Street, Tarlac City

La Maja Rica Hotel is a premier destination for comfort and elegance in Tarlac City. Featuring well-appointed rooms, a relaxing pool area, and a popular in-house restaurant, it offers a blend of modern amenities and warm hospitality. Conveniently located near major highways, business centers, and local attractions, La Maja Rica is perfect for travelers seeking both relaxation and accessibility in the heart of the city.
